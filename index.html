Think you know how to juggle loans? Prove it.
Nine Kenyans. Nine debt situations. One question: can you make the money stretch?
Each profile is a scenario drawn from the reality many borrowers navigate every month — multiple loans, limited cash, and a sequence that either works or doesn't. The catch? The order you pay matters more than the amount. Get it right and carry-forward builds. Get it wrong and the penalties arrive before you do.

Available cash shown is post-living-costs — rent, food, school fees already out. This is the allocation decision, not the income question. An AI coach breaks down every move after each round — no judgment, just the logic.
🔄 Revolving
30-day digital loans. Pay → re-borrow immediately. Miss a payment and penalties hit automatically at T+1. You exit by not re-borrowing — which requires surplus cash.
⚡ Advance
One-off cash advance. Paid once — gone permanently. No guarantee of a repeat advance; every new application goes through fresh review.
📅 Amortised
Fixed monthly instalments over 3–36 months. Each payment reduces the balance. Remaining months shown on every loan card — some close within this game.
